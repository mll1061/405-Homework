Chocolate Chip Cookies

About

This is just a recipe that most people enjoy that I pulled off the internet thanks to the amazing Betty Crocker.

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Mix:
    • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
    • 3/4 cup brown sugar
    • 1 cup butter or margarine, softened
    • 1 tsp vanilla
    • 1 egg
  3. Stir in:
    • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tsp baking soda
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • Note: Dough will be stiff
  4. Stir in chocolate chips
  5. Drop dough by rounded spoonfuls about 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet
  6. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until light brown
    • Note: Centers will be soft
  7. Cool slightly; remove from cookie sheet

Nutrition Information Per Serving (1 cookie)

Fat 8g
